Welcome to “Comic Character Source,” your number one spot to get the cinematic and literary sources for all of your favorite comic book characters!

All you have to do is type in the name of the character you would like to view the source material for and press the button “scour our resources”, then you will be given a list of options. Just click on the character that most closely matches your desired result, and you will be presented with an array of different comics/movies/tv shows that character has been in.

Once you have accomplished your goal, you can click on the “character source” button to take you back to this home screen.
